# Code of Conduct

SotuRail contributors are expected to communicate with respect, patience and technical honesty.

## Expected Behavior

- Be welcoming to new contributors.
- Assume good intent while still reviewing carefully.
- Ground claims in evidence.
- Respect user data and local-first constraints.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or discriminatory language.
- Sharing private information without consent.
- Pressuring maintainers to bypass safety or review.

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues or restrict participation when needed to keep the project healthy.
